Hamburger

Description

Burgers are such an easy option for a ketogenic diet. Most traditional burgers can be made into keto burgers just by removing the bun. Often when eating out, a bunless burger is our go to order. We have found most restaurants are happy to serve us a burger without the bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef 80/20
  • Seasonings that you prefer for your meat
  • Cheese for thy meat
  • Eggs for the topper!
  • Love to make it delicious!

Steps

  1. Cook burgers on grill or air fryer till they are to temp that is satisfactory to you.
  2. Cook eggs in bacon grease and make sure the yolks stay sort of soft.
  3. Cheese burger and put whatever other toppings you wanted.
  4. Add the egg on the top and cut it and let the yolk drain into everything for the final delicous sauce!
